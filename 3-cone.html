About this test
3-Cone Drill (L-Drill)
The 3-cone (also called the L-drill) measures multi-directional agility — start, sprint, hard cut, run a tight L, sprint home. It tests bend, hip mobility, and change of direction. Pass rushers and slot receivers benefit most; offensive linemen care less. Participation has dropped in recent Combines as some teams have de-emphasized the drill, but historical data is robust.
