Want the full picture?

Each test here scores one dimension of your fitness. The REALFIT Absolute Score combines all 13 tests into a single 0–1,000 number — the only score that tells you whether you are genuinely fitter than you were last year, regardless of your age or who you're comparing yourself to.

A 700 at 25 and a 700 at 45 mean exactly the same thing. The standard doesn't move. Only your fitness does.
