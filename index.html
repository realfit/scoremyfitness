About this scorer
First independent scorer for the official 2026 Presidential Fitness Test.

The Presidential Physical Fitness Test was reinstated by Executive Order on July 31, 2025, and the official benchmarks were published at whitehouse.gov/fitness in May 2026. This scorer implements the official Presidential Award benchmarks exactly as published, with no modifications.

The 2026 test introduces a new category-pick architecture: three categories (Core Strength, Cardiorespiratory Fitness, Upper Body Strength), each with two test options. A student picks one test per category. To earn the Presidential Award, the student must hit the age- and sex-stratified threshold for their chosen test in all three categories.

The National Award and Participation Award are part of the official Presidential Fitness Test program but their specific thresholds have not yet been publicly released by the President's Council. Both will be added to this scorer when the official thresholds are published.

Built on FITDAT

This scorer implements scoreset_us_presidential_fitness_test_2026 — a Score Set on the FITDAT v1.0 Data Specification. Six tests (test_curl_ups_1min, test_plank_hold, test_one_mile_run, test_beep_test_20m, test_right_angle_pushups, test_pullup_max) organized into three Replacement Groups (rg_pft_core_strength, rg_pft_cardio, rg_pft_upper_body) with twelve age-stratified StandardSets anchored to the new pop_us_youth_school_age_6_17 Population. Pass/fail threshold archetype with category-pick logic.
